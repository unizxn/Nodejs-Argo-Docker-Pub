Why Protect the Environment?

Our planet's ecosystems are interconnected. Small changes in our daily routines can reduce waste, conserve resources, and help lower carbon emissions to preserve biodiversity for future generations.

3 Core Actions Today

1. Conserve Energy

Unplug devices when they are not in use and rely on natural light during the day to decrease overall power consumption.

2. Reduce Single-Use Items

Switch to reusable water bottles, bags, and containers to significantly lower the amount of plastic waste sent to landfills and oceans.

3. Choose Sustainable Transit

Walk, cycle, use public transport, or carpool when possible to help decrease air pollution and personal carbon footprints.
